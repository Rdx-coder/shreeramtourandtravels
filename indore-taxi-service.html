Popular Destinations in Indore

Book a cab and explore Indore's top attractions:

  • Rajwada Palace: A stunning 18th-century palace reflecting Holkar dynasty's grandeur.
  • Khajrana Ganesh Temple: A divine place dedicated to Lord Ganesha, attracting thousands of devotees.
  • Sarafa Bazaar: A must-visit for food lovers, serving delicious street food at night.
  • Chhappan Dukan: Famous for snacks like Poha, Jalebi, and Samosa.
  • Patalpani Waterfall: A scenic spot perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Indore’s Famous Food - A Paradise for Foodies

Indore is known for its unique blend of Rajasthani, Gujarati, and Maharashtrian flavors. Here are the must-try dishes:

  • Poha Jalebi: A popular breakfast combination loved by locals.
  • Bhutte Ka Kees: A spicy corn dish that melts in the mouth.
  • Garadu: Deep-fried yam, perfect for winters.
  • Dal Bafla: A Rajasthani-style dish similar to Dal Bati.
  • Indori Namkeen: A variety of crispy snacks like Sev and Mixture.
